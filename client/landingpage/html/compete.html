How to Compete in STEM Racing

Ready to join the STEM Racing challenge? Here’s how you can get started:

  1. Check the Rules & Regulations

    The official Rules & Regulations will soon be available in the Country List section and on local websites. These guidelines are essential for ensuring your team meets all competition requirements and stays on track for success.

  2. Connect with Your Local Coordinator

    Use the Country List to find your national coordinator’s contact information. They’ll provide details on how to register and help you navigate the competition process in your region.

    3. Stay tuned for updates, and prepare to embark on your STEM Racing journey. The race to innovation starts here!
